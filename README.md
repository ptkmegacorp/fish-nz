# Fish NZ — Trout & Deep Sea Fishing Excursions in New Zealand

**Working names:** Fish NZ · FISH NEW ZEALAND · "fish new zealand"

---

## One-liner

Guided trout and deep-sea fishing tours in New Zealand for American anglers — the Alaska lodge trip Midwestern retirees already know, except the fish are in NZ and the flight is the only thing you book yourself.

---

## Positioning

| Alaska fishing tour (reference) | Fish NZ equivalent |
|--------------------------------|--------------------|
| Fly to Anchorage / Kenai / Bristol Bay | Fly to Auckland (not included) |
| Lodge + guide + boat included | Lodging, guides, boats, NZ ground transport included |
| Trout / salmon / halibut mix | Tongariro trout + Bay of Plenty deep sea |
| 7–14 day packages common | **10 / 14 / 21 day** options |
| Premium lodge pricing | **Semi-budget** — value tier to offset international airfare |
| Retiree-heavy demographic on longer trips | Same — **60+ retirees** primary for 14–21 day tours |

**Pitch to Midwestern anglers:** "You already saved for Alaska. Same trip shape — fly fishing rivers, then saltwater charters — but you're on the other side of the Pacific, the seasons flip, and we're not charging lodge-luxury rates because you already paid for the plane."

## Destinations

### Tongariro region — trout (freshwater)

- Central North Island fly fishing and spin fishing
- Tongariro River system, Lake Taupō tributaries, back-country access where permitted
- Rainbow and brown trout; classic NZ sight-fishing and nymphing
- Base: Taupō / Turangi area lodging (semi-budget: motels, holiday parks, or shared lodge — TBD)

### Mt Maunganui / Tauranga — deep sea (saltwater)

- Bay of Plenty charter fishing from Tauranga / Mt Maunganui
- Snapper, kingfish, tarakihi, seasonal tuna / marlin depending on dates
- Half- and full-day charters bundled into the tour block
- Beach town downtime between fishing days

**Route shape:** Fly into Auckland → ground transfer south to Tongariro/Taupō block → transfer north/east to Bay of Plenty → depart from Auckland (or Tauranga if routing works). All **in-country transport included** in tour price.

## What's included / excluded

### Included

- All **New Zealand ground transport** (airport pickup, inter-region transfers, charter day shuttles)
- **Accommodation** (semi-budget tier — clean, comfortable, not luxury lodge)
- **Guided fishing days** — freshwater guides + deep-sea charter days per itinerary tier
- **Meals:**
  - **2 meals/day** via **group cooking** (breakfast + lunch or breakfast + dinner — rotate by schedule; groceries provided)
  - **3 group dinners out** at local restaurants (included in price — showcase NZ food, social highlight)
- Tackle guidance / basic shared gear where applicable (confirm per charter what clients must bring)
- Trip host / coordinator on the ground

### Not included

- **International flights** (client books own — typical ex-US hub: ORD, MSP, DEN, DFW, etc.)
- Travel insurance (strongly recommended; offer as optional add-on later)
- Personal fishing licenses (NZ freshwater license — small fee, can be prepaid add-on)
- Alcohol beyond group dinner outings
- Single supplement (if applicable — price separately)
- Gratuities for guides / charter crews

## Trip length options

Comparable Alaska packages often run **7–10 days** on the water; longer "trip of a lifetime" tours hit **10–14 days**. Fish NZ starts at **10 days minimum** because clients are crossing the Pacific and hitting **two distinct fisheries**.

| Package | Length | Best for | Rough shape |
|---------|--------|----------|-------------|
| **Fish NZ 10** | 10 days / 9 nights | First-time NZ anglers, tighter vacation windows | 5 days Tongariro trout + 3 days Bay deep sea + travel/rest |
| **Fish NZ 14** | 14 days / 13 nights | **Core product** — Alaska-comparable | 6–7 trout + 4–5 deep sea + 2 flex/rest |
| **Fish NZ 21** | 21 days / 20 nights | Retirees, extended adventure | Deep immersion both regions, extra charter days, weather buffer |

**Pricing:** TBD — target **semi-budget** all-in land package (excluding flights). Benchmark against mid-tier Alaska fishing packages minus flight, then discount 15–25% vs lodge-luxury AK to reflect value positioning and group-cooking model.

**Group size:** 6–10 anglers per departure (group cooking and charter economics work best in this band).

**Season notes:** NZ trout season and offshore conditions drive calendar — publish "best months" once guides confirm (typically Oct–Apr window for combined product).

## Target demographic

### Primary — 60+ retirees (14 & 21 day tours)

- Time and budget for extended trips
- Often traveled Alaska or dreamed of it; NZ is the "next big one"
- Appreciate hosted trips — transport and meals handled
- May travel as couples or small friend groups from same fishing club
- Responsive to **trust-based sales** (referrals, phone calls, local fishing club talks)

### Secondary — 45–60 active anglers (10 & 14 day)

- Still working but using PTO for a bucket-list trip
- Midwest / Great Lakes / upper South fly-fishing culture
- Price-sensitive vs Alaska lodge; flight cost is the pain point — **semi-budget land package** is the hook

## Brand & creative

### Logo

**Primary mark:** giant wordmark — **FISH NZ** or **FISH NEW ZEALAND**

- Bold, stacked or single-line sans serif (think lodge-patch readability at distance)
- No fussy fish illustration required for v1 — the name *is* the brand
- Secondary: small "Trout · Deep Sea · New Zealand" tagline under wordmark
- Colors: deep ocean blue + river green, or monochrome for merch/embroidery

### T-shirt design

- **Front:** oversized **FISH NZ** chest print (same as logo)
- **Back:** small line art — Tongariro river + Maunganui outline map, or "Taupō → The Mount" with coordinates
- **Sleeve or hem:** year + trip name (e.g. `FISH NZ '26 — Tongariro · Maunganui`)
- Offer as **included tee for 14/21 day** bookings; upsell for 10-day and alumni

### Voice & copy tone

- Plainspoken, Midwest-friendly — no adventure-bro hype
- Emphasize: hosted, predictable, good value, real fishing days
- Avoid overpromising trophy sizes; sell experience + variety (trout *and* salt)
