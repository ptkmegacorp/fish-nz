Welcome aboard

Hosted fishing tours, good value, no fuss

Fish New Zealand is a small-group fishing holiday on the North Island. You fly to Auckland on your own schedule; from there we handle transport, lodging, guides, most meals, and three dinners out with the group. Comfortable stays, home-cooked breakfasts and lunches, and plenty of time on the water.

  • Getting here Fly into Auckland — international airfare is not part of the land package.
  • On the ground Lodging, guides, charter boats, and all NZ transport included.
  • On the water Tongariro trout fishing, then Bay of Plenty deep-sea charters.
  • Trip length Choose 10, 14, or 21 days — most guests pick 14.
  • Good company Small groups (about 6–10 anglers), two meals a day plus three group dinners out.

Where we fish

River trout, then open ocean

Auckland arrival → Taupō & Tongariro → Mt Maunganui & Tauranga → depart. Shuttles and transfers between each stop are included.

Tongariro & Taupō

World-famous trout water. Fly and spin fishing for rainbow and brown trout on the Tongariro River and Lake Taupō tributaries, with experienced local guides.

Mt Maunganui

Deep-sea charters on the Bay of Plenty — snapper, kingfish, tarakihi, and seasonal game fish. Beach-town evenings between fishing days.

More than fishing

Rest days & add-ons

Flex days on longer itineraries are perfect for unwinding between fishing blocks. Some activities are included in the rhythm of the trip; others are optional extras you can add when you book.

♨

Natural hot springs

Soak in Taupō-area thermal pools and riverside hot springs — a classic North Island treat between trout days. Included as part of the trip schedule on 14- and 21-day packages.

Optional extra
≋

White-water rafting

Add a half- or full-day raft on the Tongariro River with a licensed outfitter. Graded rapids, canyon scenery, and a splashy break from the rod. Ask when you inquire — we'll quote it with your trip.
